What is a Work Study Program?

Work-Study programs provide jobs for both graduate and undergraduate students in order to to help students with financial need. This allows students to earn money to help pay for their college expenses related to their education and/or course of study.

Bringing a Car to Campus

Typically Freshmen students are not permitted to have a car on campus, but this differs between institutions. College emphasizes off-campus transportation or areas within walking distance of the school, so investing in a bike or some other ways of transportation could be best for your campus experience. Along with that Campus Parking is another expense you must consider if you choose to bring a car. Check to see if your college offers ride passes for public transportation to save money! To find out more look up ¨Transportation Services” following the college’s name for more information.

Conclusion

No matter who you are, the college process may inflict feelings of confusion, frustration, and fear, which is totally understandable and normal. We know this is a big transition, even if you choose to attend some type of institution or to go straight into the workforce. While your decisions may change as you grow older, this website is here to provide the stepping stones of what you want to do, even if that may change along the way!
