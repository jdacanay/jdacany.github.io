MCHS College Research Resources


Our Purpose



As graduating seniors who have gone through the college process we know the stress and confusion that comes along with it. For that reason we have created multiple resources to help you choose the best path after high school for you! Our goal is to create a safe and positive environment that takes the pressure off of the future, showing you multiple routes and options you can take. Embedded in this website is everything you need to accomplish and develop your future goals, like a fun interactive explorer, scholarship and fafsa links, student advice sections, and so much more!


Our Goal In Depth


This site is designed to be a tool for younger students who are considering their futures. It is very stressful especially in the ever changing climate of education and career decisions, so this is more of a way to present multitudes of information in interesting, clear ways for young people to find options that can help them decide their next big life decision. Overall we want to create a reliable, engaging, and most importantly a low stress website that will help future generations of students pinpoint what their life will be like after high school. By providing students with guidance and support we hope to make them more prepared to take on their future; furthermore, making this process, whether college bound or not, less intimidating.


The Slideshow

This is designed to be a resource for younger classmen who are starting to consider and look into their futures. We have collected data from seniors who have recently undergone college application process in order to help guide your own research and application process. Alongside the data, seniors have also provided fruitful tips, advice, and personal experience for you to reference as you look forward to the next phase of your educational career.
